About Us

Here on Learnzilla we strive to better the experience of learning online. Through our use of software design we made this program that tracks progress and uses data gathered through interactive quizzes to increase the information retained by students. This program allows students to engage on a deeper level educational interactivity all while funding public schools as a reward of their progress. Through this process we hope educate other students and benefit their communities.



We hope that through our efforts will be able to both be able to teach students and give back to public schools that may lack sufficient funding for basic needs. We are not alone; this same vision is reflected in many places all over the world, likely even in your own backyard.
